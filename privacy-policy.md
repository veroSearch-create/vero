# Search Sanity Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

## Introduction

Search Sanity is a Chrome extension designed to improve your Google Search experience by hiding AI Overviews, demoting SEO-spam domains, boosting forum results, and verifying AI claims.

## Data Collection and Usage

Search Sanity collects **minimal data** and operates with a strong commitment to user privacy:

### What We Collect
- **Extension settings**: Your preferences for features like hiding AI Overviews, forcing Web-only results, etc.
- **Blocked/boosted domains**: Lists of domains you choose to demote or boost (stored locally)
- **No personal data**: We do not collect emails, names, IP addresses, or search queries

### How We Use Data
- Settings are stored using `chrome.storage.sync` to synchronize across your Chrome devices
- Domain lists are stored using `chrome.storage.local` for fast access
- All data remains on your devices or within Google's secure storage systems

## Data Sharing

Search Sanity does **not** share your data with any third parties:
- No analytics or telemetry
- No data sent to external servers
- No monetization of user data

## Permissions Explained

The extension requests only the minimum permissions necessary:

- `storage`: To save your preferences and domain lists
- `activeTab`: To interact with the current Google Search tab
- Host permissions for `https://www.google.com/search*` and `https://www.google.com/*`: 
  - Required to modify search result pages
  - Limited to Google search domains only

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:
- All processing happens client-side in your browser
- No external dependencies that could introduce vulnerabilities
- Regular updates to address any security concerns

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in this document.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support channel.

## International Users

If you are located outside the United States, please note that your data may be processed and stored in accordance with Google's storage systems, which may be located in various countries.

By using Search Sanity, you consent to the practices described in this privacy policy.
