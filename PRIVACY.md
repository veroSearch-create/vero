# Privacy Policy — Vero

**Last updated: May 2026**

Vero collects no data and sends no data anywhere. Ever.

- **No analytics.** Zero usage tracking of any kind.
- **No external requests.** The extension makes zero network requests outside of Google Search during normal use.
- **Local storage only.** Your settings (which features are on/off, your custom blocklist) are stored in `chrome.storage.sync`. Chrome may sync this to your Google account if you have Chrome Sync enabled — this is standard Chrome behavior outside our control.
- **No account required.** Vero works entirely on-device. There is no sign-up, no server, no account.
- **Open source.** Every line of code is auditable.

Vero reads the Google Search results page DOM to apply visual treatments. It does not read, store, transmit, or log the content of your searches or any browsing activity.

**Contact:** If you have questions, open an issue on GitHub.
