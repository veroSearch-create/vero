Vero

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Vero collects no data and sends no data anywhere. Ever.

What we collect

Nothing. There is no server, no account, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

Local storage

Your settings (which features are on/off, your custom blocklist) are stored in chrome.storage.sync. Chrome may sync this to your Google account if you have Chrome Sync enabled — this is standard Chrome behavior outside our control. We never see or access this data.

Network requests

Vero makes zero network requests to any domain other than Google Search during normal use. No telemetry, no analytics, no pings.

What the extension reads

Vero reads the Google Search results page DOM to apply visual treatments (hiding elements, dimming results, adding badges). It does not read, store, transmit, or log the content of your searches or any browsing activity.

Open source

Every line of code is auditable on GitHub. If you have questions, open an issue.
